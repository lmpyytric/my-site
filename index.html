About

Young Scholars Development Workshop

Welcome to 1st Young Scholars Development Workshop!

The First International Workshop on Young Scholars Development aims to provide a dynamic platform for young scholars to exchange ideas, share research findings, and foster collaborations in cutting-edge fields. This workshop focuses on empowering the next generation of researchers by facilitating discussions on innovative applications of artificial intelligence (AI) in key areas such as embodied intelligence, smart cities, and intelligent communications.


Scope

The workshop will explore the latest advancements and challenges in AI-driven technologies, with a special emphasis on:

  • Embodied Intelligence:
    • - AI systems that interact with the physical world, including robotics and human-machine collaboration.
  • Smart Cities:
    • - AI applications for urban development, sustainable infrastructure, and intelligent transportation.
  • Intelligent Communications:
    • - AI-enhanced networking, 5G/6G technologies, and next-generation communication systems.

By bringing together young researchers, industry experts, and academic leaders, this workshop seeks to inspire creativity, encourage interdisciplinary research, and support the professional growth of emerging scholars in the field of AI and its transformative applications.
